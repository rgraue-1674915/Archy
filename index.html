Connecting Star wars to Archaeology

For our UnEssay our group decided to use a series that we all love, that being the Star wars series to depict the themes, topics, and major ideas that were brought up throughout the class within the movies and readings we had in class.

We have decided to look into each one of the series to depict this. That being the prequals, the Original series, and the new ones. Each one of these has a major story that is protrayed and we will be follwoing that along with specific scenes and details to portray our ideas.
